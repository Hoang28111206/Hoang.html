nouns

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea

kind of nouns

Nouns are classified as follows:

  1. Proper: Its the name of a particular object, whether person, placae, animal, idea or thing
  2. Common: Its the name possessed by things in general.
  3. Collective: Its the name representing a group of things
  4. Abstract: Its the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

pronouns

A pronouns is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

kinds of pronoun

Pronouns are classified as follows:

  1. Personal pronouns: It refers to a specific noun and used as substitute of the noun
  2. Interrogative pronouns: It stands as the answer the questions about a person, place or thing.
  3. Indefinite pronouns: Its used to refer to a person, thing or place that are unknown.
  4. Relative pronouns: It relate to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.
  5. Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.
  6. Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
